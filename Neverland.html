Neverland

School project

Neverland is a 6-month end-of-study project commissioned by the Innerspace studio. We are 4 students working on it.

The aim is to study the new gameplay possibilities offered by mixed reality, and to translate them into a fifteen-minute experience. I'm working on the game design, development and asset creation for this project.

During this experience, the player will have the chance to make contact with Tinkerbell, have a snowball fight with mean snowmen, dodge the cannons of Hook's boat, and end up in a fight with our dear Captain.
