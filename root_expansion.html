Root Expansion

My role in the project :

At this Game Jam, I took on the role of Game Designer. I thought up the game mechanics: watering, planting or attacking. I designed the different plants: Cactus, Sunflower, Carnivorous Plant and Ivy and then I took care of the balancing.

I also developed part of the UI. I took care of the main menu, the settings, the credits and the game rules.
